# customer-growth-retention-analytics
This project analyzes customer lifecycle, engagement, and revenue behavior to uncover key drivers of growth and churn.  Built as a business case study, this dashboard provides actionable insights to improve retention, optimize pricing, and drive revenue.

Customer Growth & Retention Intelligence Dashboard

Analyzing Revenue, Churn, Customer Behavior & Lifetime Value

🎯 Problem Statement

Lack of visibility into the customer lifecycle
No clear link between engagement and revenue
High churn without root cause clarity
Need for a unified analytics solution

🧠 Objective

Track revenue, engagement, and churn
Identify high-value customers
Detect churn risk early
Enable data-driven decision-making

🗂️ Data Overview

The project integrates multiple datasets:
Customers → demographics & signup data
Subscriptions → plan type & lifecycle
Transactions → revenue & purchases
User Activity → engagement behavior

👉 Built using a star schema model centered on customer_id

⚙️ Data Modeling

Created Date Table for time intelligence
Established 1:M relationships
Cleaned and standardized keys
Optimized for scalable analysis

📈 Key Features

KPI Tracking (Revenue, Customers, Churn, ARPU)
Revenue trend & geographic analysis
Customer segmentation & LTV
Cohort & churn analysis
Revenue vs Activity correlation
Drill-through customer analysis

🧠 Key Insights

📈 High engagement strongly drives revenue
💰 Top customers contribute the majority of revenue (Pareto effect)
⚠️ Low activity customers have high churn risk
📉 Churn rate (~75%) is a critical business risk
🎯 Monetization gap exists in active users

🚀 Executive Takeaways

Engagement is the primary growth driver
Retention is the biggest opportunity area
High churn threatens long-term revenue
Immediate action is required

💡 Recommendations

Target high-value customers for retention
Re-engage inactive users
Optimize pricing & subscription plans
Use activity signals for churn prediction

📊 Business Impact

Improved retention strategy
Increased revenue opportunities
Better customer targeting
Data-driven decision-making
